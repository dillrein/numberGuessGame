Rules for the game derp derp

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. Tere are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden, and each time the game start the calue of each crystal will change.
